The 5 Stages of Design Thinking

1

Empathize

First of all, I reflected on the reason why I built this website. I believe the primary goal is to showcase my learning achievements and personal interests.

  • I listed four of my hobbies.
  • Reviewed my learning process and content
  • Checked the personal introduction websites of others
2

Define

Based on my research, I defined the core requirements: showcase 3+ hobbies, demonstrate design thinking, highlight accessibility features, and present a UN global goal.

  • Must include a UN global goal
  • Must document design thinking process
  • Must implement accessibility best practices
  • Must reflect personal interests and style
3

Ideate

I brainstormed various design concepts through sketching. I explored different layouts, color schemes, and navigation structures.

4

Prototype

I focused on responsive design to ensure the site works well on all devices.

5

Test

I conducted usability testing with peers. Based on feedback, I improved navigation clarity, enhanced contrast for accessibility, and simplified project descriptions.

  • Improved color contrast for better readability
  • Simplified navigation labels
  • Added hover effects for interactive elements
  • Optimized image loading times
